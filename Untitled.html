E-Stamps View

Project Details

In this Project, I'm Peter Pandey, an aspiring data analyst, and I'm taking on a challenge to analyze Telangana's growth in various sectors using data. My goal is to provide valuable insights to the Telangana government, helping them make informed decisions to boost the state's growth.

I took on the task of exploring three datasets: Stamp Registration, Transportation, and TS-iPASS. I studied their features, categories, and the time periods they cover. My analysis involved looking for trends and patterns in each department. I aimed to find opportunities for growth and areas that require attention. Additionally, I sought correlations among these departments and used insights and visual tools like shape maps to present the overall state's growth in a clear and understandable way in my presentation

Project Details

Conducted a strategic analysis for Atliq Hardware, a leading hardware production company, using SQL.
The aim was to provide swift insights for their growing data analytics team.
Utilized MySQL for robust querying, Power BI for compelling visualizations, Canva for polished presentations, and Loom for seamless video documentation.
Key findings include unique product count, peak sales figures, and overall gross sales, providing valuable insights for informed decision-making.
Recommendations focus on deepening customer insights, refining product recommendations, optimizing pricing and inventory, and identifying new product opportunities.
A project that aligns data analytics with Atliq Hardware's strategic goals.
